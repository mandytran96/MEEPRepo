Frank Ocean

Frank Ocean is an American singer, songwriter, rapper, record producer and photographer. He was born on October 28, 1987 (31 years old) in Long Beach, California. He was once part of the Los Angeles hip hip collective Odd Future. Frank Ocean frequently collaborates with Odd Future co-founder Tyler, the Creator.
